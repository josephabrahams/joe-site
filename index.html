Joseph Abrahams

Joseph Abrahams is VP of Engineering at Washington, DC-based Mariana Tek, a SaaS business management platform that powers the biggest names in the boutique fitness industry. Recognized for his team building abilities, he specializes in using a values-based leadership approach to help companies retain their culture while scaling through periods of rapid growth.

A former executive at digital agency iStrategyLabs, Joseph established the product development practice area and helped grow the technology offerings of the company, eventually leading to acquisition by WPP in 2016. At ISL he worked with a range of clients including Capital One, Facebook, Intel, NBCUniversal, Red Bull, Thomson Reuters, and Volkswagen.

Joseph currently serves on the Tech Advisory Board for the Service Year Alliance, an organization that facilities opportunities to develop real-world skills through hands-on service.
